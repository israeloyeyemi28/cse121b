    Our Story

    Welcome to McDonald's, where our story began wit two brothers, Maurice and Richard McDonald, and a vision for delicious food served fast. In the 1940's, their small resturant in Des Plaines, illinois, became the birthplace of the fast-food revolution.



    In 1955, Ray Kroc, a determined entreprenuer, saw the potential in the McDonald brothers' innovative speedeee service system. With their partnership, McDonald's expanded into a franchise, spreading accross the nation with iconic golden arches sd its symbol. From the introduction of clasics like the Big mac to adapting to changing times, McDonald's has evolved into a global cultural icon. today we continue to serve quality, affordable meals with the same dedication to innovation and excellencethat started it all.



    Join us on our journey as we strive to make every moment at McDonald's a memorable one for our customers worldwide.
